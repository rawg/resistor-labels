Resistance is Futile

(but labeling it isn't)

Do you hate rummaging through a big pile of resistors to find the value you're looking for? Try sorting and labeling them instead! The sorting is on you, but this tool will help with the labeling. For each type of resistor you've got, type out the colors of the bands. Keep each resistor on its own line, and skip the tolerance band (sorry - feel free to fork this project and add it yourself!). Once you're done, you can generate labels and download them as a PNG, fit to be printed to 8½"x11" sticker paper. For more information, read what Wikipedia has to say about Ohms and resistor color codes.
